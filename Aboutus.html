SUVIDHA - Gather to learn

A tech platform for tutors where they can monitor students growth and attentiveness in the class through online quizes and polls.

To serve user-friendly environment so that teacher can interact with each and every student and their work smoothly and effectively.

Platforms

Quiz

A tech platform for everyone.

A tech platform for everyone to increase and boost up their knowledge through regular quizes .The tutor takes the quiz after the lecture and get the responses from the attendes.

On the other hand, it provide the database of all the attentive students who had done the quiz..

Poll

A tech platform for everyone.
